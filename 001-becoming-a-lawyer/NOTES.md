## The Standard We Use For Every Paragraph in this Book

Is this the clearest, truest, and most enduring expression of the idea that we, today, know how to write?

## Originality

Is this saying something the reader is unlikely to find expressed in a similar way elsewhere?

## Our editorial standard

1. Is it true?

Could we defend it?

2. Is it clear?

Could a first-year law student understand it?

3. Is it timeless?

Will it still matter decades from now?

4. Is it memorable?

Will one sentence stay with the reader?

5. Is it distinctive?

Does it express something in a way the reader is unlikely to encounter elsewhere?
