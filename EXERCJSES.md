# Exercises

## Exercise 1

If you happen to see a freshly filed civil case, quietly look for the Summons to Enter Appearance and see:

1. What does it look like?
2. Who has signed it?
3. Does it bear the court seal?
4. Is it attached to the Plaint?

Question:
Why do you think the law requires a Summons to Enter Appearance in addition to a Plaint?
