1. Keep your notes in the same format for **every legal** document:

- **Definition** – What is it?
- **Purpose** – Why does it exist?
- **Contents** – What does it contain?
- **Who prepares it?**
- **Who signs it?**
- **When is it used?**
- **Why is it important?**

2. Whenever you pick up a court file, ask yourself just one question:

_"Why does this document exist?"_

Not:

_"What is this document called?"_

But:

_"What legal problem is this document solving?"_
